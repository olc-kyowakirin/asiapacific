Speak Up Line

Kyowa Kirin is an organization committed to the highest ethical standards with core values of integrity, innovation and teamwork (Wa). You may read our Code of Conduct on Kyowa Kirin’s global website.


At Kyowa Kirin, we uphold a zero tolerance against unethical conduct and encourage speaking up on concerns or reporting of unethical behavior or action. We are dedicated to providing everyone working for or with our organization a direct channel to voice concern or reporting, should they observe any impropriety during the course of working with our organization, especially if the matter pertains to suspected breach of laws, regulations or compliance standards.


Reporting of such matters can be done through this “Speak Up” line , hosted by EthicsPoint, our third-party provider. We encourage all who work or engage with us to report any matter of concern freely and openly at the earliest possible opportunity.


The submissions through EthicsPoint will be conveyed to us in a strictly confidential and, if preferred, anonymous manner. We are committed to addressing your concerns and taking appropriate action.

Do not use this line for the following:


  • For reporting by employees of any adverse events following use of Kyowa Kirin products or associated product quality complaints, please report to the Local Safety Officer.
  • For reporting by external parties of any adverse events following use of Kyowa Kirin products or associated product quality complaints, please see local Kyowa Kirin websites for contact details.
